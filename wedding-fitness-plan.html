Dinner — Salmon + Quinoa + Mushroom Veggie Sauce
~30 min prep · cook quinoa in bulk for the week
  • 6oz / 170g / 5oz / 142g Member's Mark Wild-Caught Alaskan Salmon (thawed from frozen · bake or pan-sear) 310/259 kcal · 36/30g P · 2g C · 18/15g F
  • 150g cooked / 100g cooked Quinoa (measure after cooking · cook in bulk for the week) 183/122 kcal · 7/4.5g P · 33/22g C · 3/2g F
  • 100g Organic Baby Bella Mushrooms (from Sam's · sautéed in spray oil) 22 kcal · 3g P · 4g C · 0g F
  • 60g Red Onion (from Sam's · diced and sautéed with mushrooms) 24 kcal · 0.5g P · 5.5g C · 0g F
  • 60g Sweet Bell Pepper (from Sam's multi-pack · diced) 19 kcal · 0.6g P · 4.5g C · 0.1g F
  • spray 0-calorie Spray Oil (for cooking mushrooms · 5 sprays max) ~10 kcal · 0g P · 0g C · 1g F
🏋️ 568 kcal · 47g P · 49g C · 22g F 👑 462 kcal · 39g P · 39g C · 18g F
💡 Quinoa (Grubanny method — youtube.com/watch?v=FDGt5ElvIPc): Add quinoa to a bowl, pour cold water in and rub vigorously between your palms until the water goes cloudy — this removes bitterness. Strain through a fine mesh strainer. Add to pot with 1.5 cups water per 2 cups quinoa + a pinch of salt. Cover and boil exactly 3 min, then reduce to a simmer for 12 min. Fluff with a fork. Result: perfectly fluffy, zero clumps. Cook a full batch Sunday and portion into containers — 150g cooked for Mayowa · 100g cooked for Vanessa.

💡 Salmon: Preheat oven to 400°F. Season with garlic powder, paprika, salt. Bake 12–15 min until it flakes easily.

💡 Mushroom sauce: Sauté baby bella + red onion + bell pepper in spray oil over medium heat until soft, ~8 min. Season with garlic powder and black pepper. Spoon over salmon and quinoa.

⚖️ Strict vs. Flex Guide Mon–Fri Strict · Sat–Sun Flex
⚡ Strict Days (Mon–Fri)
●Hit all 4 meals exactly as planned — weigh your portions
●No dessert unless you executed every meal AND completed your workout
●Hit your step goal (10k for Vanessa · 10k for Mayowa)
●Take all supplements without skipping
●Log your weight every morning (fasted, after bathroom)
●Swap ingredients using the substitution guide — stay in same macro range
●Water: aim for 1 gallon (Mayowa) / 3 liters (Vanessa)
🌟 Flex Days (Sat–Sun)
●Still hit breakfast, lunch, and snack on plan
●Dinner can be a free meal — keep it under ~700 kcal
●Still do your workout (leg day Sunday is non-negotiable)
●Plantains are a great flex carb — bake or air-fry instead of frying
●If you go over — don't spiral. Just get back on Monday
●Zucchini bread + Halo Top is your go-to flex dessert — have it guilt-free
●One flex meal is NOT a cheat day — keep it clean overall
🔄 Food Substitutions (Same-Box Rule)
You can swap proteins for proteins (e.g. salmon → chicken, ground beef → ground turkey 93%), carbs for carbs (quinoa → rice → plantain), and fats for fats (PB → almond butter → avocado). Always pick from the same macro category and keep portions weight-equivalent. Never swap a fat for a carb or vice versa — it'll throw your macros off.
